Our Story

A little jewel,
hiding in plain sight

Le Bijou means "the jewel" — and that's exactly the feeling we chase every morning behind our glass case on Palace Street. The oven starts before the street wakes up: croissants go in pale and come out glossed and flaking, chicken and fish pies get stuffed generously, and the espresso machine hasn't really stopped hissing since we opened.

Neighbours started calling us their happy place long before Google did — we're proud to carry a 4.4-star name in North Kaneshie, one warm pastry at a time.
